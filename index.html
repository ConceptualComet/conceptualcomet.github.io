Colophon (noun): an inscription at the end of a book or manuscript usually with facts about its production. Definition from Merriam-Webster.com Dictionary.

Colophon

Name

Like a comet, my interests burn bright and move fast for a short time before they fade into inky black distance, but always return once more on their orbiting path. That point when a comet reaches its shortest distance to the sun is called the perihelion.

Design

I wanted to capture the idea of things collected in a folder/journal over long stretches of time by an entity observing the Earth and delighting in human culture. Of course, space-related items are of particular interest. I purposely rejected 'simple' design and 'y2k nostalgia-bait' and embraced my own style of eclectic maximalism to make a site that feels fully me.

Technology

This is the first website I've fully built myself since high school (class of 07!), so I kept it very simple on the technology side. I handcode directly on GitHub in the browser. Yep, even the blog entries. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. The music player is Amplitude.js. My domain name registrar is Porkbun. I don't track analytics or use cookies, etc. The less I know about you, the better.
